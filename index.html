What we do

Independent, client-side advisory.

Captiva Consulting works on one side of the table: yours. We represent the client's interest through complex IT and ERP programs — never as the system integrator. ERP implementation is where that independence matters most, and where our record is sharpest: multiple projects delivered on time and on budget across both SaaS ERP and SAP for the Government of Canada.

  • 01

    Scope & SOW stewardship

    We hold the line on scope, review change orders before they land, and keep the statement of work honest as the program evolves.

  • 02

    Division-of-labor framework

    Clear boundaries between client and integrator, established early — so responsibilities never fall through the gap mid-build.

  • 03

    End-to-end testing & cutover readiness

    We prepare your team for end-to-end testing, data validation, and a controlled go-live rather than a hopeful one.

  • 04

    Decision & risk support

    An experienced counterpart to a seasoned SI, so first-time client decisions on configuration and the chart of accounts are made with eyes open.

  • 05

    Security assessment & authorization (SA&A)

  • 06

    Hypercare & transition

    We plan the handover to steady-state operations and the train-the-trainer path that lets your people own the system after the SI leaves.

Martin is the Principal Consultant at Captiva Consulting, where he represents organizations as the owner's advisor through complex IT and ERP programs. His focus is client-side professional services — supporting and managing implementations from the buyer's seat, independent of the system integrator delivering the work.

He has delivered multiple ERP projects on time and on budget for the Government of Canada, across both SaaS ERP and SAP. His value is the experienced counterpart a first-time client needs when sitting across from a seasoned integrator: someone who knows where programs drift, how scope erodes, and how to keep the engagement constructive without giving ground that matters.
